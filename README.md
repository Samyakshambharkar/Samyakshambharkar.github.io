# Typing Speed Tester
Test your typing speed with typing speed tester !

A Typing Speed Tester, built with HTML, CSS and JS is a simple Typing Speed Tester where you can test yourself how fast you type and by testing yourself regularly will help you to boost your typing speed and will help to do your pc works faster than others. 

In this repo, I will be putting extra focus on setting Attributes in JavaScript. If you want to learn how to use setAttritube and getAttribute effectively, please have a look at my code.

I have kept the styling at a bare miniumum for you to go wild and make it your own. Please tag me as I would LOVE to see your game!!!

## What inbuilt functions are we going to focus on?
- loops
- DOM manipulation
- addEventListener
- setAttribute
- getAttribute
- data-name
- document.querySelector
- createElement

## Rules of Typing Speed Tester
- You will start by clicking on the start button or in text area.
- Then you have to type the given words correctly.
- The test will end in 1 minute.
- At the end, the results will be showm.
- In results, there will be your typing speed, accuracy and the errors you made.
- It is good for enhancing your speed even if you dont have much time.
